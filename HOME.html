About

This digital archive is a curated collection of visual material documenting the early years of Maison Margiela, spanning from 1989 to 1996. Rather than functioning as a traditional runway archive, this project brings together a broader range of imagery. It includes not only documented runway looks, but also found media: behind the scenes photographs, garment details and other visual fragments. The intention of this archive is to capture a visual view of the brands early identity that extends beyond finished collections and into the spaces, materials and contexts that shaped them.
